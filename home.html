About

Fat Cow is committed to quality. We grind our own blend of 100% natural Angus beef every day. We hand cut and fry our fries to order. We only use fresh ground pepper and kosher salt to season our burgers and fries. We are proud to use fresh and local premium ingredients when possible. We make all our sauces and dressings fresh in house.
