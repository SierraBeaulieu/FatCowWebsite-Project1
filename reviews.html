  • 2/5 Stars

    • 4 years ago, I gave this place a 5 star review after a half dozen visits because the food and the service were both excellent. However this trip, was quite different. Dirty tables everywhere, sloppily assembled food, and mediocre execution on the burgers. The staff was friendly enough and accommodating, but it feels like management stopped caring at some point. I typically will give places the benefit of the doubt, and understand that Covid has made running a restaurant significantly more challenging. That being said there is a basic standard that I think places need to meet, especially if I’m going to drop $45 on some crappy burgers. With all of the other great burger options we probably won’t be back. I hope they get things turned around because we used to really like this place.
